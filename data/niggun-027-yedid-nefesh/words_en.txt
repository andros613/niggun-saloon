Beloved of the soul,
Compassionate Father,
draw Your servant
to Your will.
Then Your servant will hurry
like a deer
to bow
before Your majesty.
To him will be sweeter -
Your friendship -
than the dripping of the honeycomb
and all taste.

Majestic, beautiful,
radiance of the universe
my soul is sick
for your love.
Please O G‑d,
heal her now
by showing her
the pleasantness of Your radiance.
Then she will be strengthened
and healed
and hers will be
eternal gladness.

All worthy One, may be aroused
Your mercy
and please take pity
on the son of Your beloved,
because it is so very long
that I have yearned intensely
to see the splendor
of Your strength,
only these, did desire
my heart
so please take pity
and do not conceal Yourself.

Please be revealed
and spread upon me, my Beloved,
the shelter of
Your peace.
Illuminate the world
with your glory
that we may rejoice
and be glad with You.
Hasten, be loved,
for the time has come,
and show us grace
as in days of old.
