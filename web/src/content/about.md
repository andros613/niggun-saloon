# About Niggun Saloon

**Niggun Saloon** is a free, open collection of Jewish music arrangements: sheet music, audio recordings, and lyrics, built to make it easy to learn, teach, and share niggunim.

May our learning and singing of niggunim hasten the imminent *Geulah Shleimah* (complete redemption), the coming of *Moshiach Tzidkeinu*, and the rebuilding of the *Beit Hamikdash* in Yerushalayim.
